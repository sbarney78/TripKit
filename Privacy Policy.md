# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  
Owner: Steven

## Overview
TripKit and Fuel_Log_Book are personal-use mobile applications designed for private, offline data storage. These apps do not collect, transmit, or share personal information unless explicitly stated below.

## Data Collection
The apps store data locally on your device. Examples include:
- trip logs  
- fuel entries  
- notes  
- images you choose to attach  
- settings and preferences  

No data is automatically uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any external service unless you manually configure a sync server.

## Data Storage
All data is stored locally on your device. You are responsible for maintaining backups and preventing data loss.

## Third‑Party Services
These apps do not use analytics, advertising networks, or third‑party tracking services.

If you configure a custom backend or sync server, your data may be transmitted to that server. The privacy of that server is your responsibility.

## Permissions
The apps may request optional permissions such as:
- Storage (to save images or backups)
- Camera (to attach photos)

These permissions are used only for the features you explicitly choose to use.

## Children’s Privacy
These apps are not intended for children under 13. No personal data is knowingly collected.

## Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the apps indicates acceptance of any changes.

## Contact
For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the owner directly.
